## Why I'm Starting to Write

There's a certain magic to putting words on a page and hitting publish. For years I kept notes — scribbled ideas in notebooks, drafts abandoned in Google Docs, half-formed thoughts in Notion. None of them ever made it out into the open.

This blog is my attempt to change that.

## What to Expect

I plan to write about things I'm learning, things I'm building, and things I'm thinking about. Roughly:

- **Work and craft** — notes from the day job, lessons learned the hard way
- **Reading** — books worth talking about, passages worth sharing
- **Ideas** — half-baked and otherwise

I'm not going for volume. I'd rather publish one genuinely useful thing a month than ten posts that say nothing.

## A Note on Format

Posts here will vary in length. Some will be quick observations — a paragraph or two. Others will be longer explorations when a topic earns it.

I'll try to be clear, specific, and honest. If I'm wrong about something, I'll update the post and say so.

## Let's Go

Thanks for reading this far. If any of this sounds interesting, stick around. I'll be publishing regularly starting now.
